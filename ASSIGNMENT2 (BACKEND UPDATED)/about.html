Biography

Prof. Ts. Dr. Nor Azman Ismail is a distinguished academic with over 20 years of experience in the field of Human-Computer Interaction and Computer Science.

Currently serving as a Professor in Human-Computer Interaction at the Faculty of Computing, Universiti Teknologi Malaysia (UTM), Dr. Nor Azman has established himself as a leading researcher in the region. His academic journey spans multiple continents and prestigious institutions, bringing a wealth of international perspective to his work.

As Deputy Director (Digital Media) and University Web Director at UTM, he plays a pivotal role in shaping the digital landscape of the university. His leadership extends beyond administrative duties to mentoring the next generation of researchers and practitioners in HCI and related fields.

Dr. Nor Azman's research interests are diverse yet interconnected, focusing on creating technology that truly serves human needs. From developing emotion-aware dialogue systems to exploring computer vision applications in education, his work consistently bridges the gap between cutting-edge technology and practical human benefit.

His contributions have been recognized through numerous awards, invited talks at international conferences, and successful collaborations with industry partners including Infinite Loop Media and Guangzhou KEO. He continues to push the boundaries of HCI research while maintaining a strong commitment to teaching and knowledge dissemination.
