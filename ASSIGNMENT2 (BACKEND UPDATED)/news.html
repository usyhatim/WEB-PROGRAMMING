News Timeline

Jan 15 2024
Award

Best Paper Award at International Conference on HCI 2024

I am honored to receive the Best Paper Award at the International Conference on Human-Computer Interaction 2024 for our research on emotion-aware interface design. This recognition highlights the importance of affective computing in creating more responsive and empathetic technology.

The paper titled "A Framework for Emotion-Aware Dialogue Systems in Educational Contexts" was selected from over 200 submissions. The award was presented at the conference opening ceremony in Copenhagen, Denmark.

Dec 08 2023
Collaboration

Partnership with Infinite Loop Media

We are excited to announce a new industry collaboration with Infinite Loop Media, a leading creative technology company. This partnership will focus on immersive user experience research and interactive media design projects.

The collaboration will involve joint research projects, student internships, and knowledge transfer programs. Initial projects will explore the application of emotion recognition technology in interactive storytelling and virtual reality experiences.

Nov 22 2023
Invited Talk

Keynote Speaker at Guangzhou KEO Summit

I was honored to deliver a keynote address at the Guangzhou KEO Summit on "The Future of AI-Driven Human-Computer Interaction" to an international audience of researchers, industry professionals, and policymakers.

The summit brought together over 500 participants from 30 countries. My presentation focused on emerging trends in AI-powered interfaces, the ethical considerations of emotion-aware systems, and the potential impact on education and healthcare sectors.

Oct 15 2023
Workshop

Workshop on UX Design for Accessibility

Conducted a two-day intensive workshop on designing accessible user interfaces at the Faculty of Computing, UTM. The workshop attracted 50 participants from academia and industry.

Participants learned practical techniques for designing inclusive digital experiences, including screen reader compatibility, keyboard navigation, color contrast considerations, and assistive technology integration.

Sep 05 2023
Award

Excellence in Research Award from UTM

Received the Excellence in Research Award from Universiti Teknologi Malaysia in recognition of outstanding research output and impact in the field of Human-Computer Interaction.

The award acknowledges sustained research productivity, successful grant acquisitions, and significant contributions to the academic community through publications and collaborative research initiatives.

Aug 18 2023
Collaboration

MoU Signing with Guangzhou KEO

A Memorandum of Understanding was signed between UTM and Guangzhou KEO to establish a framework for joint research, student exchange, and technology transfer in the areas of AI and HCI.

The partnership will facilitate collaborative research projects, joint publications, and the development of innovative solutions for smart city applications and intelligent systems.

Jul 12 2023
Invited Talk

Seminar at Loughborough University

Delivered an invited seminar at my alma mater, Loughborough University, on "Two Decades of HCI Research: Reflections and Future Directions."

The seminar provided an opportunity to reconnect with former colleagues and share insights from 20 years of research in Human-Computer Interaction, with particular emphasis on the evolution of user-centered design methodologies.

AI in Education Workshop Series

Launched a workshop series on AI applications in education, bringing together educators, technologists, and policymakers to explore the potential of AI in transforming learning experiences.

The series includes hands-on sessions on implementing AI tools in classrooms, ethical considerations, and strategies for ensuring equitable access to AI-enhanced education.
