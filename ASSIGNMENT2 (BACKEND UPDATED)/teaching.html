Teaching Philosophy

I believe in creating an engaging, inclusive learning environment where students develop both theoretical understanding and practical skills.

My teaching approach emphasizes hands-on learning, real-world applications, and critical thinking. I strive to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry practice, preparing students for successful careers in technology and research.

Through project-based learning, collaborative activities, and continuous feedback, I aim to foster a deep understanding of Human-Computer Interaction and related fields while nurturing creativity and innovation.

Undergraduate Courses

SECJ/SECV 3xxx

Human-Computer Interaction

Fundamentals of HCI including user-centered design, usability principles, prototyping, and evaluation methods. Students complete a semester-long design project.

Key Topics:

  • User-centered design process
  • Usability heuristics and principles
  • Prototyping techniques
  • User research methods
  • Usability testing and evaluation
Course Materials
SECJ/SECV 3xxx

User Experience Design

Comprehensive exploration of UX design principles, research methods, and design tools. Focus on creating meaningful user experiences across digital platforms.

Key Topics:

  • UX research and user personas
  • Information architecture
  • Interaction design patterns
  • Visual design principles
  • Design systems and accessibility
Course Materials
SECJ/SECV 3xxx

Web Usability and Analytics

Study of web usability principles and analytics techniques for measuring and improving website performance and user satisfaction.

Key Topics:

  • Web usability guidelines
  • Analytics tools and metrics
  • A/B testing and optimization
  • Conversion rate optimization
  • User behavior analysis
Course Materials
SECJ/SECV 4xxx

Mobile Interaction Design

Design principles and patterns for mobile applications. Covers platform-specific guidelines, touch interaction, and responsive design.

Key Topics:

  • Mobile design patterns
  • iOS and Android guidelines
  • Touch interaction design
  • Responsive and adaptive design
  • Mobile usability testing
