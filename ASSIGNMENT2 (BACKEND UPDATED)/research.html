Research Areas

My research focuses on creating technology that enhances human capabilities and experiences. Through interdisciplinary approaches, I explore the intersection of computer science, psychology, and design to develop innovative solutions for real-world challenges.

Detailed Research Areas

Human-Computer Interaction (HCI)

Human-Computer Interaction research focuses on designing intuitive, efficient, and enjoyable interfaces that bridge the gap between humans and technology. My work in this area encompasses user experience design, usability engineering, and interaction design methodologies.

Key Focus Areas:

  • User-centered design methodologies
  • Usability evaluation and testing
  • Interface design patterns and guidelines
  • Accessibility and inclusive design
  • Cross-cultural interface design

Current Projects:

Adaptive Interface Design for Elderly Users

Developing age-appropriate interfaces that adapt to cognitive and physical capabilities of older adults.

Cultural Usability Framework

Creating guidelines for designing interfaces that respect and accommodate cultural differences.

Computer Vision

Computer Vision research enables machines to interpret and understand visual information from the world. My work focuses on developing algorithms for real-time image and video analysis with applications in education, healthcare, and human-computer interaction.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Facial expression recognition
  • Object detection and tracking
  • Gesture recognition
  • Scene understanding
  • Real-time video processing

Current Projects:

Real-Time Emotion Recognition in E-Learning

Using computer vision to detect student engagement and emotional states during online learning sessions.

Vision-Based Accessibility Tools

Developing computer vision systems to assist visually impaired users in navigating digital and physical environments.

Emotion-Aware Dialogue Systems

Emotion-aware systems represent the next generation of human-computer interaction, where technology can perceive, understand, and respond to human emotions. This research combines natural language processing, affective computing, and dialogue management.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Affective computing and emotion detection
  • Context-aware dialogue management
  • Multimodal emotion recognition
  • Empathetic response generation
  • Conversational AI design

Current Projects:

Empathetic Virtual Tutor

Developing an AI tutor that adapts its teaching style based on student's emotional state and learning progress.

Mental Health Support Chatbot

Creating a dialogue system that provides emotional support and early intervention for mental health concerns.

AI Applications in Education & Healthcare

Artificial Intelligence has transformative potential in education and healthcare. My research explores how AI can personalize learning experiences, improve healthcare delivery, and create more accessible services for diverse populations.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Personalized learning systems
  • Intelligent tutoring systems
  • Healthcare monitoring and prediction
  • Medical image analysis
  • Health behavior change interventions

Current Projects:

AI-Powered Adaptive Learning Platform

Building a learning management system that adapts content difficulty and presentation style based on individual student needs.

Remote Patient Monitoring System

Developing AI algorithms for continuous health monitoring and early warning systems for chronic conditions.
